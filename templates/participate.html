Welcome to the Inside Out project!

To participate, please send an email to insideoutapp@gmail.com with a Full Size Vertical Portrait (the highest resolution possible on your phone) and the following information:

 1. First AND Last Name
 2. City, Country
 3. Your Statement (what you stand for/care about)

By sending this email you agree to give the Inside Out project permission to upload and use this portrait and agree to terms of service.

We will reply to you by email so that you can continue the process and receive your poster!

If you want to organize an Inside Out Group Action, please send us an email to jr@insideoutproject.net

Thanks for participating in a Global Art Project!
